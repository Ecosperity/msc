Know More About Us

MSC Technology India is the technology development arm of the largest shipping and logistics titan in the world – the MSC Group with headquarters at Geneva. MSC Technology India, established in the year 2007, provides exclusive maritime software-based services and technology consultancy to MSC globally.
As a technology driven IT services company our focus has been to place our energies on building innovatively advanced and secure IT services, technology infrastructure training, building capabilities and up-skilling our human capital to propel project efficiencies for timely delivery of projects. We are engaged in the development of unique ERP solutions with two key facets; attending to the technology needs of the principal headquarters and providing are internal stakeholder companies with new-age solutions. And this is has been made possible because we care for our people and our people have been the cornerstone in achieving our strategic goals.
